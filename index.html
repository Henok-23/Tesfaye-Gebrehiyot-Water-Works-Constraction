Tesfaye Gebrehiyot water works construction General contractor (TG-WWCGC)

is a private limited company established in 2017 and has its headquarters in Debre Birhan, Ethiopia. TG-WWCGC has undertaken various projects in Ethiopia. It is one of the construction firms in Ethiopia in construction sector i.e. Construction of rural and urban water supply, sanitation and sewerage works, Irrigation and drainage work, flood control structure, construction of office and residence, construction of garden, pump and generator room, Construction of toilet.
